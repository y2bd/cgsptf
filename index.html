Apps.

The things that I create are things that I see a need for. I've been making applications for my phone and computer for the last four years, and my projects have ranged from bookmark managers (Pinned) to journaling applications (Sofa) to niche encyclopedias (SmogonWP).

My applications are very much for me first, but I've made things that others use as well. SmogonWP had a team of 30 beta-testers during its development, and maintains 4.7 average rating with over 500 reviews worldwide.

Nearly all of my projects are open-source, and available on my GitHub.

About me.

My name is Jason Lo. I'm currently a Computer Science student going to University of California, San Diego. I grew up though in San Francisco.

When it comes to development, I find the most passion in making things that I would personally enjoy using, whether it be applications or games. I'm not a designer by any means (nor do I pretend to be), but I enjoy working on front-end development. Being able to change the way people directly act with their computers is an extremely exciting prospect to me.
